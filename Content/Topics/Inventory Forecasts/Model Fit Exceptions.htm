Model fit exceptions

The Model Fit exception flags level forecasts where the MAPE (Mean Average Percentage Error) calculated by the Forecast engine is less than the specified threshold for acceptable model fit.

The following example shows a forecast with a model fit of only 7.08%.

Filter conditions

To filter for forecasts with Model Fit exceptions, select:

    • Demand Planning > Level Forecast - Method Fit is smaller or equal to 30 (or whatever percentage value is to be the threshold).
    • Level Forecast Exceptions > Level Forecast Reviewed is False.
