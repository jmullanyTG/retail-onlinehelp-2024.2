Forecast method and method fit

The forecast method name displayed represents the tournament winner and its respective method fit (based on the error method used). For more information, see Error method overview.

Use the Forecast Method dropdown list to view the results of the forecast tournament for that particular item. The results are ordered by their respective method fit scores. Any method with a result of 0.00% is considered to have been disqualified from the tournament and the reason for its disqualification is listed in the Forecast Message field.

Forecast parameters display similar information to the details tab, but gives an opportunity to compare the different methods at a glance.

The Forecast Method Fit is a percentage value indicating how well a forecast method matches sales history. As part of the forecast tournament process, a method fit array is generated starting with the first sales history value and progressively determining what the forecast would have been with the selected method.
