Lost sales exceptions

The Lost Sales exception flags forecasts when lost sales due to stock outs are detected in a previous period.

Filter conditions

To filter for forecasts with Lost Sales exceptions, select:

    • Inventory Forecast Exceptions > Inventory Forecast Lost Sales Detection is True.
    • Level Forecast Exceptions > Level Forecast Reviewed is False.
