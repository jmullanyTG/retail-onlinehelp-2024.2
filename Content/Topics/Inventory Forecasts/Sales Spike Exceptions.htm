Sales spike exceptions

The Sales Spike exception flags level forecasts when sales in a past period greatly exceeded the forecasted demand for that period.

Filter conditions

To filter for forecasts with Sales Spike exceptions:

Create a new filter and configure the following from the Level Forecast Exceptions metric set:

    • Level Forecast Sales Spike Factor is more than 4 (or whatever value is to be the threshold).
    • Level Forecast Reviewed is False.
