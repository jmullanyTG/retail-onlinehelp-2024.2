Construction

Chart construction contains details about how the chart is built.

Appearance – The appearance section lets users set the color palette and the style, as well as to preview their graph.

Series – The series section displays all of the included data series for the chart. Depending on the chart type, there may be one or many series displayed at one time.

Data – The data section describes the data points returned by the method to the chart.
