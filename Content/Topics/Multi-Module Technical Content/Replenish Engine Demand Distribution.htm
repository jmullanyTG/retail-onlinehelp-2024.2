Replenish engine demand distribution

The Replenish Engine uses split factors to even out demand distribution.

Background

With the Replenish Engine, over and under ordering issues were encountered due to inaccurate distribution of demand (these were particularly problematic with Monthly buckets and short Lead Time Days). Decimals were rounded to fulfill order multiple and issue quantities (up or down) which further exacerbated the over and under ordering activity within the Replenish Engine.

Current Behavior

Whenever demand is read into the Replenish Engine, it is broken down into daily demand. The mechanism for splitting down demand has changed, as the Replenish Engine has moved from front-loading demand to now doing a more even demand split.

Result

The Replenish Engine produces more accurate results based on the intended modeling resulting in recommended orders inline with expectations while reducing the over and under order scenarios. Recommended Orders coming out of the Replenish Engine are easier to understand as a result.
