Creating a weekly recurring schedule

Weekly recurring schedules are used to control ordering behavior based on a day of the week.

To create a weekly recurring schedule

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Schedules.

    The Schedule Set screen displays.

  2. Select the schedule set to add the new schedule to.

    A new schedule row is added under the selected schedule set.

  3. Click Add.

  4. On the right side of the screen, enter the schedule name and description.
  5. Select Recurring from the Schedule Type dropdown menu.
  6. From the Occurs dropdown menus in the Frequency section, select Weekly.
  7. Choose a frequency from the dropdown menu in the Recurs every field.
  8. Select a day of the week.
  9. In the Duration section, enter the start- and end dates or select No end date if the schedule’s duration is unknown.
  10. Click Save.
