Creating a daily recurring schedule

Daily recurring schedules are used to control ordering behavior on a non-weekly or monthly basis. The recurrence frequency repeats based on individual days.

To create a daily recurring schedule

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Schedules.

    The Schedule Set screen displays.

  2. Select the schedule set to add the new schedule to.

    A new schedule row is added under the selected schedule set.

  3. Click Add.

  4. On the right side of the screen, enter the schedule name and description.
  5. From the Schedule Type dropdown menu, select Recurring.

  6. From the respective dropdown menus in the Frequency section, select the schedule (daily, weekly, etc) and day frequencies.
  7. Click Save.
