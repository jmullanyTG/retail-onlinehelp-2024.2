Creating a monthly schedule

Monthly schedules are created based on a numerical day or a day of the week. For five-week months, the last modifier covers the final day of the week during the month. During four-week months, fourth and last resolve to the same day.

By default, weeks start on Sundays.

To create a monthly schedule

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Schedules.

    The Schedule Set screen displays.

  2. Select the schedule set to add to the new schedule, and click Add.

    A new schedule row is added under the selected schedule set.

  3. On the right side of the screen, enter the schedule name and description.
  4. Choose Recurring from the Schedule Type dropdown menu.
  5. Select Monthly from the Occurs dropdown menu in the Frequency section.
  6. Select either the Day or The radio buttons and complete the options as required.
  7. In the Duration section, enter the start- and end dates or select No end date if the schedule’s duration is unknown.
  8. Click Save.

The frequency can be changed to every other month by changing the month(s) modifier to 2.
