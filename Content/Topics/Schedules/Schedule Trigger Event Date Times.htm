Viewing schedule details

Schedules can be reviewed in the summary view on the schedule page. Select a range start and end date to view all of the affected dates bound by the range. The default range end is one week from today’s date. This may require adjustment to view long running trigger events.

Viewing schedule details

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Schedules.

    The Schedule Set screen displays.

  2. Select a schedule to view.

  3. From the right panel, click Details.

    The Schedule Trigger Event Date Times dialog displays the details of the schedule including affected dates.
