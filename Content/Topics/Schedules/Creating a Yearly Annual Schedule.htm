Creating a yearly (annual) schedule

Annual schedules are based on a selected numerical day of the year, the first day of a specified month, or on a selected date. For all annual schedules, the frequency can be set to occur every year, every other year, or with extended frequency.

Any schedule with a day value greater than 60 is affected by leap years.

The count starts on January 1st.

To create a yearly schedule

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Schedules.

    The Schedule Set screen displays.

  2. Select the schedule set to add to the new schedule, and click Add.

    A new schedule row is added under the selected schedule set.

  3. On the right side of the screen, enter the schedule name and description.
  4. From the Schedule Type dropdown menu, select Recurring.

  5. From the Occurs dropdown menu in the Frequency section, select Yearly.

  6. Select the appropriate radio button and complete the fields as required.
  7. In the Duration section, enter the start- and end dates or select No end date if the schedule’s duration is unknown.
  8. Click Save.
