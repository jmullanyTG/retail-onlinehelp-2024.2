Allocations

Gaining insight as to why an order didn't happen

Users now have a quick and convenient way to investigate why an order from a selected Allocation Strategy didn't happen. With the insight the new Health Check tab provides, users can resolve certain issues independently and quickly, reducing the need to escalate support tickets and the time to resolution.

The new Health Check tab can be found on the Allocation Strategy view.

Steps and conditions

There are four possible conditions:

Condition color Description
Green Good
Yellow Caution
Red Bad (the report provides manual check suggestions)
Grey Blocked (check was skipped)

Each step has possible conditions:

Step Name Possible Condition Reason
Gathering Data Good, Bad Please check Allocation Strategies exception status to resolve any exceptions.
Forecast Good, Bad No forecast generated. Could be strategy is phased out, improper product model used or no demand based on demand type.
Forecast Retrend Good, Caution, Bad No forecast retrend generated. Could be not enough data points to generate a retrend or system policy is misconfigured.
Calculation Good, Caution, Bad No calculation was done. Contact support to check the status of system configurations.
Finalizing Orders Good, Bad, Blocked Check site priorities or prepack overage %.
