Assortment Planning

Extended multiple currency planning

TG JE Assortment Planning v2024.1 separated out AUT into Home AUT and Blended AUT as part of an effort to better support planning in multiple currencies. TG JE v2024.2 extends this functionality, the Blended AUT metric has been added to the:

  • Historical section on the Hindsight Management screen.

  • Image view (View Options > Quick Text drop-down menu).
