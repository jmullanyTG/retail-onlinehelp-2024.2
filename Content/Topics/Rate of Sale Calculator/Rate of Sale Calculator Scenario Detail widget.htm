Reviewing the performance of items used in the ROS calculation

Use the Scenario Detail supporting view to review the performance of items used in the ROS calculation. The Scenario Detailview displays all items that were used in the calculation and where they fall in the performance range. Within the view, items can be selectively excluded from calculations.

To exclude an item from the calculation, uncheck the item’s Included field and click Save.

Undocking the Scenario Detail widget

To undock the Scenario Detail Widget from the right hand side of the screen:

  1. Click on the Scenario Detail button on the right side of the screen.

  2. Right-click on the Scenario Detail heading and select Float.

    3. The Scenario Detail widget can now be moved around and resized.
