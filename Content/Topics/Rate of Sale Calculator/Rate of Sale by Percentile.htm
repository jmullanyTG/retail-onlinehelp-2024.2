Rate of Sale by Percentile

After scenarios have been calculated, the results are grouped into the following performance percentiles (configurable during implementation):

  • Minimum
  • 25th percentile
  • Median (50th percentile)
  • 75th percentile
  • Maximum
  • Average (default selection)

Average shows the true average rate of sale per variant/period for the selected data, not the average from the configured percentile bands

The sales history for the selected items is distributed across a performance percentile (Worst, 30%, 50%, 70%, Best).

Column definitions

  • Raw ROS: No data cleansing or filtering
  • Adjusted ROS: Sales history that has been cleansed by the data cleansing steps outlined previously in this document
  • Record Count: The number of data points (style/color sales periods) used in the calculation of the displayed rates of sale – to serve as an indication of relative data strength
  • Effective ROS: The ROS to be applied to the list of placeholders/variants in the third and bottom view of the ROS calculator screen.  The Effective ROS can be adjusted by checking the ROS from the Scenario results or by manually entering a value.  ROS selection is cluster specific and the distribution selection can vary by cluster
