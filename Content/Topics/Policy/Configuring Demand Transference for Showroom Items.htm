Configuring demand transference for showroom items

Showroom items are sold in showrooms (locations where the item is sold but not fulfilled).

To configure showroom settings

  1. From the Execute tab, click Policy.

  2. Click Edit.

  3. From the Order Policy Type dropdown menu on the Policy section, select Showroom.
  4. Make the necessary changes in the remaining sections, as follows:
    • Minimum Safety Stock Units: the minimum number of units to display in the showroom.
    • Designated Fulfillment Site: the designated site as the fulfillment location for showroom sales.
    • Demand Transference Method: if showroom orders can be fulfilled from multiple locations, select Split Factor.
  5. Click Ok.
