Negative normal and event causals

The behavior of a negative value in types Causal and Event is controlled through the system policy Ignore Negative Causals (Engine: Forecasts), which defaults to No.

If set to Yes, the causal application logic ignores negative causals as it refers to the slope of the causal factor in relation to the sales data series. Thus, negative causal factors are allowed to have negative effects (but not positive effects) when the option is set.
