Reviewing new store forecasts

After forecasts have been generated for a new store, either manually or automatically as part of an overnight process, review the forecasts for items in the store.

To review a forecast for a new store

  1. From the Management tab, click Inventory Manager.

    The Items window displays.

  2. From the Filter dropdown menu, select a filter, and click Ok.

    The Choose Fields window displays.

  3. From the Choose Fields window, select fields as required.

  4. Click Ok.

    The Inventory Manager Document displays.

  5. Filter the Site Name to display the new store.
  6. Double-click on an item to display its details pane.
  7. In the Details pane, click the Forecast Viewer tab to see the demand forecast for the item.
  8. Repeat these steps for other items at the site until satisfied with the demand forecasting for the new store.

  9. If further revisions are required, modify the store supersession and any related item supersessions as necessary.

    The new site may not have been added to an item supersession, resulting in no sales or forecasts for the item at the store.
