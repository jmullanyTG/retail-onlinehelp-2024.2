Shipping and receiving schedules

Schedules help determine when orders need to be placed so that inventory gets to sites in time to meet demand.

In Site Manager, multiple types of schedules can be assigned to a site.

To assign a schedule to a site

  1. From the Management tab, click Site Manager. The Items window displays.

  2. From the Filter dropdown menu, select a filter.

  3. Click Choose on the toolbar.

  4. In the Choose Fields dialog, navigate to Site Schedules and select the schedule type.
  5. Click Ok.
  6. In the Site Manager Document, locate the appropriate row.

  7. Click in the Schedule column and select the appropriate schedule.

  8. Click Save.

    The schedule assignment is saved.
