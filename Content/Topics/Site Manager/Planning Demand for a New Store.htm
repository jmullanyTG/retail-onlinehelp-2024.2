Planning demand for new stores

The process of planning demand for new stores spans site, supply chain, and item management to ensure that the sites, SKUs, supply chains, and model stores are set up to accurately forecast demand for the new stores.

Process

Stage Requirement Task
1. Identify new stores and confirm opening dates Confirming open dates for new sites
2. Validate SKUs ranged to new stores  
3. Ensure new stores are in supply chains Supply chain overview
4. Create store supersessions Creating site model options and details
5. Review demand forecasts for the new stores Reviewing New Store Forecasts
6. Manage item supersessions as necessary Using supersessions to introduce new items into stores
