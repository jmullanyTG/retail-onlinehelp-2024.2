Reserving stock at a distribution center

Stock can be reserved in a distribution center (DC) for use by specific stores and channels. For example, reserving stock for a new store ensures stock is available when it opens or reserving stock for a specific sales channel such as e-commerce.

Reserved stock is logically partitioned to hide it from other sites or channels. This also prevents it from influencing normal order quantities for other sites and channels.

The actual amount of stock reserved for a store or channel is demand-driven and can vary from store-to-store and item-to-item based on:

  • how many days of stock to reserve.
  • whether to include safety stock in the reservation.
  • the forecasted demand for the item at that location.

Reservations-related fields

The following fields support stock reservations. For easy management and visibility of stock reservation settings, add them as columns in Site Manager documents.

  • Reserve? – Whether stock should be reserved for a store or channel.
  • Days Reserved – The number of days of demand to cover when calculating how much stock to reserve.
  • Consider Presentation? – Whether safety stock (Safety Stock Days and Minimum Safety Stock Units values) should be included when calculating how much stock to reserve.
  • Is Contained in Location Strategy? – Read-only. Displays whether the site is part of a location strategy.

Default values for these fields can be set by store in Site Manager. If a site is part of a location strategy, the default values can be overridden in the Allocation module's Location Exceptions view.

Reserving stock for placeholder sites, stores, or channels

Stock for placeholder sites and regular stores or channels can be reserved.

To reserve stock at a DC

  1. From the Management tab, click Site Manager.
  2. Click Site Manager Document in the Site Manager toolbar.
  3. Select a site filter that contains the sites to reserve stock for.
  4. In the Columns toolbar, click Choose.

  5. Include the following Allocations fields:

    • Reserve?
    • Days Reserved
    • Consider Presentation?

    • Is Contained in Location Strategy

  6. In the Site Manager document, find a store to reserve stock for. Enter values for it in the following columns:

    Column Description
    Reserve?

    If checked, then stock is reserved for this store. Click the checkbox to toggle checking and un-checking the checkbox.

    Days Reserved

    The number of days of stock to reserve.

    The forecasted demand for each item at the selected site is used to calculate the quantity of stock required to meet this number of days of demand. Exact quantities of items reserved vary according to the forecasted demand for each item.
    Consider Presentation?

    Whether stock is included to cover safety stock requirements (specified in each item's Safety Stock Days and Minimum Safety Stock Units values) in its reservation calculations.

    If checked, then enough stock is reserved to cover the safety stock requirements for each item.

  7. Click Save.

    How much stock is required for the reservation is calculated and then orders generated as needed to ensure enough stock is available to the site when needed (such a new site's phase-in date).

    The values entered in Site Manager can be overridden in the Allocations module if a site is part of a location strategy. For more information, see Allocations Strategy Location Exception View.For more information, refer to the Allocations User Guide.
