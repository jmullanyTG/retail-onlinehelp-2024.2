Selecting content to update

If a product is included in a future Promotion or Event, when the Master Content has been updated in the Product Content pane and changes saved, the Select Content to Update window displays.

Both Promotion and Event content can be updated:

  • Promotion Content – Future Promotions containing the product. Selecting Promotions overwrites the Promotion content with the updated Master Content.
  • Event Content – Future Events containing Promotions with the product. Selecting Events overwrites the Event content for the Promotion with the updated Master Content.

Click Ok to save and update the selected content.
