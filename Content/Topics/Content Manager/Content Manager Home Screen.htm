Content Manager home screen

The Content Manager home screen displays four panes that provide the ability to manage products, product groups and their respective Master Content.

Whether Content Manager is configured for Item level or Variant level is determined by system policy Promotion Product Selection Level.

Screen panes

  • Products – (Top left) Displays all Item Masters or Variants.

  • Groups – (Bottom left) Displays all groupings of products.

    Double clicking on a product group displays both the Product Content and Group panes.

    Use the Edit button to edit Item Master groups, Variant groups, and groupings of groups or double click the product in the Product Groups pane.

  • Product Content – Displays all Master Content versions of text and images for products.

    Each Master Content version may have unique fields.

  • Group – Displays when a group is edited or by double clicking it in the Products pane or Groups pane respectively.

    The contents of this pane varies depending on the type of product selected. If an Item Master is selected in the Products pane, the Group pane is hidden as there are no child products that are associated to it.

    Click View Items to display the associated child products.
