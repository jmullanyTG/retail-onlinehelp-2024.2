Managing notifications using the Notification Inbox

Notifications are viewed, sorted, and dismissed using the Notification Inbox. Notifications that are no longer relevant can be dismissed to remove them from immediate view. The dismissed notifications are stored under History on the Notification Inbox screen.

The Notification Inbox can be moved to another screen notifications sorted by time, solution, and type.

To sort notifications

  1. In any screen, click the Notification icon on the right side at the top of the screen.

    The Notification Inbox displays.

  2. From the Sort By dropdown list, select how to sort notifications: by Time, Solution, or Type.

    The notifications display in the selected sorted order.

To dismiss a notification from any screen

  • When the notification displays on the screen, click the X icon.

    The notification is dismissed and disappears from the screen.

To dismiss a notification in the Notification inbox

In the Notification Inbox, do one of the following:

  • To dismiss all notifications at once, click Dismiss All.
  • To dismiss a single notification, click the red icon next to the notification.

To view a dismissed notification

  • In the Notification Inbox, click History.

    The dismissed notifications display.
