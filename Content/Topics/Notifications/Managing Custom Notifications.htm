Managing custom notifications

Only new notifications that are not on the History tab can be updated.

To update a custom notification

  1. From the Maintenance solution, click Notification Maintenance.

    The Notification Maintenance screen displays.

  2. Select the custom notification to update.

    The Notification Properties panel displays the notification details.

  3. Make the necessary changes.
  4. Click Save to update the custom notification.

To delete a custom notification

  1. From the Notification Maintenance screen, select the custom notification delete.

    The Notification Properties panel displays the notification details.

  2. Click Delete.

    A confirmation message displays.

  3. Click Ok to delete the custom notification.
  4. Click Save.
