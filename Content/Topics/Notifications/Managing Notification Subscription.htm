Managing notifications

Notifications are managed using the Notification Maintenance screen. Users can be subscribed to all or to a set of core and custom notifications, along with their defaults delivery method. The screen is divided into 3 areas:

  • a left pane where the reason for the notification is selected
  • a middle pane where the type of notification is selected
  • a properties section to the right that displays how the notification is configured

By default, the system marks any subscribed notification with a Soft delivery method. For details, see Notification Delivery Methods.

The delivery method is changed by selecting a different checkbox against the relevant notification type.

To subscribe to notifications

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Notification Maintenance.
  2. Select the checkbox at the module level (for example, Allocation) to subscribe to all notifications for that module or select individual notification checkboxes.
  3. Choose a delivery method.
  4. Click Save.
