The Notification Inbox

Notifications report when processes (e.g. calculations, forecasts, validations, etc.) have been initiated, canceled, expired, failed, or completed and if any errors occurred. Notification delivery settings are system configured, but these settings can be changed on the Notification Preferences screen.

The notification icon also displays the number of unread notifications.

To access the Notification Inbox

  • Click the flag icon on the right side at the top of any screen.
