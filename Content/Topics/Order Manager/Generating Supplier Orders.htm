Generating supplier orders

Supplier orders are orders the application recommends placing with suppliers to ensure there is enough inventory at sites to meet anticipated demand. In a typical supply chain, supplier orders are used to stock DCs. Distribution orders can then be generated to distribute inventory to stores.

For more information, see Generating distribution orders.

Setup

The following objects should be set up before generating supplier orders:

  • a filter that includes the items and sites to generate orders for.
  • the supply chain to use.
  • demand forecasts for the items to distribute.
  • stocking strategies for the items to distribute.

To generate a supplier order

  1. From the Execute tab, click Order Manager.
  2. In the Order toolbar, click Supplier.

  3. In the Items dialog box, select the filter with the items and sites to generate orders for.

  4. In the Add Supplier Order dialog box, enter the parameters for the order.

  5. Click Ok.

     The recommended orders are calculated and display on the screen. Each row represents a separate recommended order, and contains basic details of the order.

Double-click rows to view the demand forecast, stocking strategy, stock holding projection, and other details that drove the order recommendation.

Alternatively, click the Details button to view or hide the details section.

Results

The order follows a company's authorization workflow. Once authorized, the order is sent to an ERP for processing.
