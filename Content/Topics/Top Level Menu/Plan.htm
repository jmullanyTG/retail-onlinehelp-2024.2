Plan

Planning is comprised of the following depending on licenses and user privileges.

Merchandise Financial Planning: displays a plan approval workflow with an innovative approach to data management and user interaction. From this tab, create, approve, and manage financial plans.

Assortment Planning: after the plan framework is created, fine tune at multiple levels and apply supplemental analysis from integrated supporting modules to craft an ideal buy strategy. From this tab, plan promotions, manage assortment plans, track plan accuracy and make adjustments in-season.

Target Choice Count: a stand-alone version of the powerful Target Creation tool within the Assortment Planning module. This gives users the ability to take advantage of intelligent recommendations when creating assortments even if they do not use the Assortment Planning module.

Wholesale Planning: TG JE wholesale-related functionality structured as a logical progression through the planning and execution processes.
