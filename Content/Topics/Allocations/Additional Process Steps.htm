Supporting functionality that affects how Allocations functions

Certain ancillary features that affect how Allocations functions are found in other modules, including Site Clustering, Supply Chain, Policy, Site Manager, and Inventory Manager.

Order Manager

After allocation strategies have been fully formulated, the orders must be executed in Order Manager (replenishment and allocations) as From Strategy allocation orders. Here, orders are organized and processed for approval and export.

Inventory Policy

Order multiple values are updated and maintained through the Policy module or at an inventory (item-site) level in Inventory Manager. Order multiple values are applied at the time of order calculation and are reflected in the Constrained Demand metric within the Allocation Rollup view.

Site Manager

Store and DC ordering and receiving schedules are set and maintained in Site Manager.

  • Receiving Schedule – the days on which a store or DC may receive delivered stock. Orders are not generated for any day on which stock is not received into the location. Typically applied to stores.
  • Shipping Schedule – the days on which a store or DC may ship stock out of the location. Orders will not be recommended for shipment on any day on which stock may not be shipped from a source location. Typically applied to DCs.

Schedule sets must be configured in the Schedules module. Shipping and receiving schedules can also be applied at the inventory (item-location) level in the Inventory Manager. Inventory level schedules override site level schedule values as they are considered an override.
