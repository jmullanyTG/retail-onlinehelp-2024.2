Clearing substitutions and other size model overrides

Any overrides made to a site’s size curve, including size curve substitutions, can be cleared reverting the curve back to its originally calculated values.

In the bottom grid of the Size Models screen:

  1. Check the Clear Override field of any site where the field is highlighted in orange.

    Orange indicates an override is present.

  2. Click Save.

The contribution values of the site now revert back to their original calculated values. Any Override Sites that were assigned to the sites are cleared.

Any sites that were assigned the site that had its overrides cleared as an Override Site and also had the Sync Override box checked revert back to the Override Site’s original values.

Changes to the Is Eligible? checkboxes of a site that had overrides cleared revert to their original state.
