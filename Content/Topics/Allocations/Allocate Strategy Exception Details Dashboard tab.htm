Allocation Strategy Exceptions Details dashboard

The Allocate Strategy Exception Details [Dashboard] tab displays any item exceptions related to the strategy using pie charts.

Click on the pie segments to filter the data.

Section descriptions

  • Exception Percentage – All of the inventory (item-site combination) level exceptions within the strategy. The percentages of the exceptions within the strategy are represented within the pie chart.
  • Site Exception Areas – After the exception type has been selected, the site groupings at which the exception is occurring displays in the Site Exception Areas field.
  • Site Details – The inventory level exception for the specific site or sites.
