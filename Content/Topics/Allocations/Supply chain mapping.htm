Supply chain mapping

Supply chain mapping is controlled through the Supply Chain Map Configuration maintenance and configuration view. Both Ongoing Item and Replenishment strategy selection types share a supply chain.

The default supply chain for Adaptive Allocations is also controlled within this maintenance view. Values within the Supply Chain Map Configuration view override any default values within a system policy (e.g. Allocations Default Active Supply Chain).

It’s important to note if there is a different supply chain mapped to new items versus ongoing items. Also, ensure that the correct AllocateStrategyName has been selected for each Allocation Strategy in which they are generating orders to stores.
