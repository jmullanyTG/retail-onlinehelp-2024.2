Supply chain configuration

Allocations supply chains are configured within the Supply Chain module.

After configuration, the desired supply chain must be selected for use by:

  • listing it as the default active supply chain in the Allocations Default Active Supply Chain system policy

  • listing it as the preferred supply chain based on strategy type in the Supply Chain view

  • or both. 

For more information, see Supply Chain Overview.

For more information, refer to the Supply Chain User Guide.
