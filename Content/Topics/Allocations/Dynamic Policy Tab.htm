Using inventory policies to manage minimum safety stock

Dynamic safety stock policies associated with Allocation Strategies display in the Dynamic Policy blade in the Allocation Strategy view. This blade has two sections: Inventory and Policy.

Inventory policies can be used to manage minimum safety stock within Allocation Strategies. Using dynamic minimum safety stock policy values for allocation managed items more effectively manages both minimum safety stock values over time and safety stock at the SKU-location level.

An Allocation Strategy can have multiple dynamic safety stock policies for different time frames with all the policies being taken into account. The Allocation Strategy goes back to the normal stock holding pattern once a policy's end date has been reached. Navigate to the Stock Holding Projection blade in Inventor Manager to see the impact of dynamic safety stock policies.

The dynamic policy values are managed in the Policy module.

The Allocation Strategy grid has a column labeled Dynamic SS Policies. The checkbox in this column provides a convenient way to quickly scroll through the displayed strategies to find strategies that have associated dynamic safety stock policies. If the checkbox is checked, then there are associated dynamic safety stock policies. If unchecked, there are no dynamic safety stock policies.

Inventory section

The Inventory section displays information relating to the item/site. Only inventories with dynamic safety stock policies display. Inventories that do not have dynamic safety stock policies do not display.

Policy section

The Policy section displays the dynamic minimum safety stock units for that inventory.
