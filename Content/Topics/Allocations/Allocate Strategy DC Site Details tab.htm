Stock holding information for distribution centers

The Allocate Strategy DC Site Details tab displays stock holding information for the sites which have been designated as distribution centers as part of the setup configuration. This information can be used to help drive the formation of allocation strategies.

A grid displays distribution center level details relating to strategies.

DC level weeks of cover values are managed by entering a value in the WOC field and clicking Save.

A detailed read-only Item Master (size) level breakdown is also available.
