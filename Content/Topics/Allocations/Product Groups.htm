Product Groups screen

The Product Groups screen is used to distribute inventory outside of the product hierarchy. Users can allocate hierarchy independent collections or sets or deal with mismatched and excess inventory for clearance purposes.

A Product Group is a user configured collection of strategies with each strategy associated with one or more channels. Each product group is represented as a single strategy line within the Allocation Strategy screen. Product Groups with no included strategies do not display on the Allocation Strategy screen.

The lower left section of the Product Groups screen displays strategies based on the selected filter. Strategies that are within a Product Group do not display on the Allocation Strategy screen. However, the Product Group with these strategies does display on the Allocation Strategy screen as a strategy line.

A strategy cannot be part of more than one Product Group. If a strategy is selected that is already included in another Product Group, then once Save is clicked the strategy is automatically removed from the other group and included in the group currently worked with.

However, a Level ID can have multiple strategies. The different strategies can be included in different Product Groups.

Creating Product Groups

  1. Click New.

    A new line is added at the bottom of the grid.

  2. Enter a group name.
  3. Optionally, enter a group description.
  4. Click the ellipsis within the Product Eligibility field.
  5. Use the Items dialog to select an Item Master filter.
  6. In the Group Allocation Priority field, click the dropdown arrow and select a priority.
  7. Click Save on the top toolbar.
  8. In the bottom left grid, check the Include checkbox to add strategies to the Product Group.

  9. Click Save on the toolbar of the lower left grid.
  10. Click Execute.
