Allocation rollup filter parameters

Several filter parameters must be passed to quantify the contents of the rollup.

  • Item Filter – Item master level filter used to determine the products included in the rollup.
  • Product Hierarchy – The included levels of the product hierarchy to which users can roll up the data. Defined by a system policy.
  • Hierarchy Level – Level within the product hierarchy to which data is aggregated.
  • Location Hierarchy – Location hierarchy specific to the business. Typically reflective of the full company location hierarchy.
  • Location Level – Level within the location hierarchy to which data is aggregated.

  • Time Select – Allows users to determine the time period for the data they wish to view.

    The time periods returned also depends on the Allocations Rollup Weeks of Sales History and Allocations Rollup Weeks of Projection system policy values.

  • Calculation – Selects the calculation type for all selected parameter values.

    • Total Company – For all specified sites, locations, and periods, includes the sum of values in the aggregations.

      Total company values are not a rolling sum.

    • Average Store – Divides the total company aggregation values by the site count.
