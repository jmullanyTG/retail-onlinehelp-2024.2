Site contribution grid fields and descriptions

The site contribution grid at the bottom of the Product Model tab displays the percent contribution to the total from each of the sites. Depending on the highlighted product model, the contribution per site to the product model can be reviewed. The data can be pivoted and contribution viewed as a graph.

  • SiteName – The name of the site.
  • SiteCode – Unique identifier of the site.
  • SiteCodeName - An aggregation of SiteCode and SiteName.
  • Hierarchy – Site specific hierarchy information.
  • Contribution – Percentage contribution for each site.
  • Raw Contribution Sort – A helper field used for data sorting. Values are irrelevant to contribution calculations.
