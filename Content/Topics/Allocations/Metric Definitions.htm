Allocation rollup metric definitions

Metric Definition Business Value
Lost Sales Calculated missed sales opportunities based on potential out of stocks Allocators have visibility to missed sales opportunities as a result of potential out of stock situations. This is the system’s best estimate of foregone and missed sales.
Optimal Allocation Total Recommended Allocation - constrained by available inventory and any/all user specified constraints – includes recommended safety stock if there any. Based on all user-specified constraints, the system recommends an optimal allocation value to allocators.
Receipts Actual Orders received in stores Users can see how much stock stores have received.
Recommended Safety Stock Safety Stock Rollup (Safety Stock Units or WOC by store – sum of the greater of the two by size/store). The value may change each week as it represents the portion of the allocation that covers safety stock. Based on the calculation, this value is the recommended value for safety stock as part of the allocation.
Reserved Stock Any stock which has been reserved and is not available for allocation Reserved stock (whether for specific events or purposes, new stores or items, etc.) and not available to allocate.
Site Count Total store count in Location Strategy (DC not Included) Based on the configured location strategy, allocators can see how many sites are included within the allocation roll-up.
Stock On Hand Beginning of Week SOH – actual for past and current weeks, projected OH for future weeks Allocators will know what the stock on hand at each site is.
Supplier Orders Total Orders (Receipts + In Transit) Allocators can know what stock will be received from supplier orders giving them a good idea of incoming orders at the source-site level.
Total Actionable Stock Displays for both Stores and DCs but is more meaningful for DCs. Reflects the available to Allocate in the beginning of the current week. For stores, this number matches the Stock On Hand field. For DCs, this number may differ from stock on hand if there is pending stock (i.e. an allocation has been created against the DC inventory). Total Actionable is the maximum value that Available to Allocate should be and is exclusive of constraints. This gives allocators a good idea of how much stock they will be able to allocate.
Unit Sales (actual) Actual Sales Quantity by week – will display historically Allocators will be able to see their actual historic sales.
