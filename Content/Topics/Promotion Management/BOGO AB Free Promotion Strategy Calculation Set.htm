BOGO AB free promotion strategy calculation set

Since the EDLP value is always available for each Item in the system, it is not necessary to fill down this information from the Promotion level to the Item level. Fill down the keyed buy quantity and get quantity from the Promotion to all levels.

For a BOGO AB Promotion Strategy, Calculations must be performed for the Buy Group and the Get Group. The Item level LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice are set to the EDLP. Roll up LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

  1. * BOGO AB - Filldown - Buy Quantity - Item
  2. ** BOGO AB - Filldown - Buy Quantity - Variant
  3. * BOGO AB - Filldown - Get Quantity - Item
  4. ** BOGO AB - Filldown - Get Quantity - Variant
  5. BOGO AB Free - Promo Low -  Item
  6. BOGO AB Free - Promo High -  Item
  7. **Standard - Promo Low - Variant
  8. **Standard - Promo High - Variant
  9. Standard - Promo Low - Group
  10. Standard - Promo High - Group
  11. Standard - Promo Low - Promo Aggr
  12. Standard - Promo High - Promo Aggr

*Item fill down Calculation is only included in an Item Master implementation and can be omitted for a Variant implementation.

**Variant Calculations are only included in a Variant implementation and can be omitted for an Item Master implementation.
