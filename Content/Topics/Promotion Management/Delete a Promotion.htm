Deleting promotions

With the appropriate rights, promotions can be deleted for the current promotion status. Once deleted, all promotion level site clusters, product groups, filters, pricing, copy and images linked to the promotion are permanently deleted.

There are four ways to delete a promotion:

  • promotions Home Screen – List
    1. From the List tab, select the checkbox next to the promotion to delete.
    2. Click on Delete.
    3. Click Yes to delete the promotion.
  • promotions Home Screen – promotion Calendar
    1. From the promotion Calendar tab, select the promotion to delete.
    2. Click on Delete.
    3. Click Yes to delete the promotion.
  • promotions Home Screen – Item / Variant Calendar
    1. From the Item / Variant Calendar tab, select the promotion to delete.
    2. Click on Delete.
    3. Click Yes to delete the promotion.
  • promotion Detail
    1. From within the promotion itself, click on Delete at the top of the screen.
    2. Click Yes to delete the promotion.
