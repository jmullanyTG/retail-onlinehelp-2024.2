Promotion calendars

There are two Gantt chart views within the Promotion module that display scheduled promotions. The:

  • Promotion Calendar tab displays all promotions.

  • the Item Master tab or Variant Calendar tab is a view with promotions against products over time.

Double click a promotion within a calendar to open the promotion in a separate tab.

The Time Scale buttons on the toolbar can be used to view promotions by time of day, day, week, month, quarter or year. Filter can be used to filter the list of Promotions as in the List tab.

Right-click within the chart to display various menu options.
