Adding products to BOGO promotion strategies

Selecting a Buy Get Type strategy generates a Create New Promotion wizard that is somewhat different to the wizard that displays when Promoted Price or Promoted Discount type strategy is selected. Products and groups are added to the Buy Group and Get Group instead of being added to the Promotion Group.

BOGO AA and BOGO AB promotions

BOGO AA

For BOGO AA Promotions, Buy and Get Groups are the same Group. Buy and Get groups are identical and assigned under Buy Group.

Buy 1 of Item A, B, or C and Get 1 of Item A, B, or C at no extra cost. However, if the Get group is a subset of the Buy group (only A and C are available), then this would be a BOGO AB scenario.

BOGO AB

For BOGO AB, Buy and Get Groups are different Groups. A Buy Group contains groups of Items that must be purchased to receive whatever groups are specified under the accompanying Get Group.

Buy any 2 of Items A, B, and C (under the Buy Group) and get a $5 Gift Card (under the Get Group).

Editing BOGO AA groups

  1. Click Edit in the Buy Get Groups pane to display the Edit Buy Get Group window:

  2. Highlight the record against the blue trolley icon in the Buy Group.

  3. Click Edit to display the Edit Buy Group window.
  4. Add and remove products and groups as required.
  5. Enter the quantity and any other information required for the Buy Group.
  6. Click Close.

Editing BOGO AB groups

Buy Group

  1. Highlight the record against the blue trolley icon in the Buy Group.

  2. Click Edit to display the Edit Buy Group window.
  3. Add and remove products and groups as required.
  4. Enter the quantity and any other information required for the Buy Group.
  5. Click Ok.
  6. Click Close.

Get Group

  1. Highlight the record against the blue handbag icon in the Get Group.

    The Edit Get Group window displays.

  2. Click on Edit.

  3. Add and remove products and groups as required.
  4. Enter the quantity and any other information required for the Get Group.
  5. Click Ok then click Close.

Click Close after the Buy and Get Groups have been set up. The pricing information is entered after the Promotion is saved.
