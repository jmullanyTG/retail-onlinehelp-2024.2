BOGO AA free promotion strategy calculation set

Since the EDLP value is always available for each Item in the system, it is not necessary to fill down this information from the Promotion level to the Item level. Fill down the keyed buy quantity and get quantity from the Promotion to all levels.

Set the Item level LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice equal to the EDLP. Roll up LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

In the Promotion Strategy, the Is Group Synced checkbox is marked to ensure Buy and Get Calculations are performed on all products in the Promotion.

  12. Standard - Promo High - Promo Aggr

*Item fill down Calculation is only included in an Item Master implementation and can be omitted for a Variant implementation.

**Variant Calculations are only included in a Variant implementation and can be omitted for an Item Master implementation.
