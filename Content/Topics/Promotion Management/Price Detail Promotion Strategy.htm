Price details

The Price Details tab varies depending on the Promotion Strategy selected. The grid in the left pane is used to enter Promotion level pricing that is the default for all products in the Site Cluster. Changes are applied to the pivot grid in the right pane upon clicking Save.

The pivot grid displays customized columns based on the specified Promotion Strategy. This grid provides the ability to override pricing for one or more products for the specified Site Cluster. Click the Fields option to add or remove columns.

Pricing overrides at the product level for the Site Cluster are made in the right pane.
