Remove products from a site cluster

To remove item masters or variants from a promotion site cluster:

  1. Navigate to Item Group under Promotion Group to display the individual products.
  2. Select the checkbox next to the appropriate products.
  3. Click Remove.

  4. Click Yes to confirm removal.

    The updates are automatically saved to the promotion.

To remove one or more products from item groups or variant groups from the promotion:

Highlighting the promotion group and clicking Remove deletes all SKUs from the promotion. Be very cautious of performing this action.

  1. Navigate to the product group to modify under Promotion Group.
  2. Click Edit to display the Edit Group window.
  3. Select the product group with the dates of the promotion.
  4. Click the black hammer icon to modify the promotion filter.
  5. Select Update The Following Filter.
  6. Ensure the filter with the dates of the promotion is selected and click Ok.

  7. Modify the filter as desired and click Ok.

    The item group or variant group updates.
