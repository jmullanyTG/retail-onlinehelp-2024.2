Companion items

The Companion Items tab provides the ability to promote products that are not included in the Promotion. Some examples of Companion Items are batteries for promoted electronic devices, extended warranties, cell phone cases and car chargers. Companion Item associations are independent of Site Cluster definitions.

To add a Companion Item, click on Add to display the products Filter window. Select from an existing filter or click on the black hammer icon to create a custom filter.

Click Ok when finished selecting products. The Companion Items are added to the grid. Comments can be added directly into the grid if needed.
