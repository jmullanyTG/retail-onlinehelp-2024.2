Content update window

The Content Update window displays when Save is clicked after the promotion content has been updated and the promotion has been placed on a future Event or the product in the promotion has Master Content.

Promotion content such as copy text, images, or both can be pushed to the following:

  • Master Content – A promotion can be updated with the promotion content if it consists of a single Product or Product Group that exists in Content Manager.
  • Event Content – If the promotion is added to any future Events, they are listed and which Events should be updated with the promotion content can be selected.

Click Ok to save changes.
