Attribute management

Adaptive Allocations is reliant on the existence of product attributes to make informed decisions. Therefore, basic product attributes are required with increasing capabilities based on the availability of additional attributes.

Product attributes can be imported (along with their relationships to products) from an external system or created and managed within Attribute Manager if otherwise unavailable.

For additional information, see Attribute Management and Assignment Overview.

For additional information, refer to the Attribute Management User Guide.
