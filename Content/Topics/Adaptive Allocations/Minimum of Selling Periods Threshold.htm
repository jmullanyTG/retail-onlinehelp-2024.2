Minimum % of Selling Periods Threshold calculation

Compares the remaining qualified periods against the total count of selling periods in the specified time frame, then qualifies the variant if it meets or exceeds the threshold.

In the following, the weeks 1-7 have been set as the time frame. The sales volume % of max threshold value has been set to 15% and minimum % of selling periods threshold has been set to 50%.

Given the threshold, weeks 4-7 are disqualified. The requirement to meet 50% of qualified periods is not met. Therefore, the variant is disqualified from the choice count calculation.
