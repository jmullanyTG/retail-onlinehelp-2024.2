Hybrid plan based demand plans

In Location Planning, financial targets are created for levels of the product hierarchy down to store level. The creation of a hybrid plan based demand plan, within Adaptive Allocations, is similar to that of strategic financial planning. However, there is no need for a product model as store level indexing is not necessary.
