Technical details about supersessions

Supersession information is contained in the following three tables (note the spelling):

  • tbl_SupercessionHeader: holds the information visible in the Supersession Grid.
  • tbl_SupercessionInventory: holds the inventory links.
  • tbl_SupercessionSite: holds the site-specific replenishment types for the old items.

Linking a supersession

For the forecasting engine to read the demand history from a linked supersession, the data is written to tbl_InventoryHistory in the SupercessionHistoryQuantity column. If there are lost sales in the superseded (old item) history, they come into tbl_InventoryHistory as SupercessionLostSalesQuantity. These may then be included or excluded at the user’s discretion along with regular lost sales quantities.

Data is copied for all time periods which fall within the “Sales History Start Date” and “Sales History End Date” window.
