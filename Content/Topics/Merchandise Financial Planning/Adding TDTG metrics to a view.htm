Adding period to-date and to-goal metrics to views

To add Period To-Date and To-Goal metrics to a view:

  1. Click Fields in the view toolbar.

  2. Drag the desired period TD\TG metrics to the Column Area of the layout.

  3. Click Ok.

    The aggregate TD\TG columns display in the view.
