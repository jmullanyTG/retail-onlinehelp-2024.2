Balancing to high-level targets using weighted splits

One approach to planning within MFP is working to get all lowest level values properly tiered and then entering high-level targets at the end. Effectively, this rebalances the entire plan while maintaining the overall structure of the work already done.

Example of balancing

When period level or aggregate level values beneath must be kept intact, users can use this method in tandem with Lock & Spread functionality to change only unlocked cells to achieve a $120,000 target.

For more information, see Lock & Spread.

For sales across the US and the UK, across multiple channels and brands, the original sales target is $94,322 across the board for 2017_m07.

The sales target is revised to $120,000.

By entering the desired target value of $120,000 in the Grand Total row for 2017_m07, the sales ratios are maintained at every level from Country to Brand.
