MFP related job tasks

The MFP tasks are primarily used to actualize history & roll the current inventory, take snapshots, and clean up deleted plans.

  1. (MFP) (Daily) Maintain MFP Snapshots: Determines which snapshot templates are ready to have a snapshot taken, and takes the snapshot.

  2. (MFP) (Weekly) Maintenance: Clean up deleted plans: Purges data from financial plans that have been deleted.

  3. (MFP) (Weekly) Maintain Hybrid Based Plans: Updates the MFP Hybrid tables with the latest actualized data. Requires configuration of the job task parameters. The job task parameters can be useful for troubleshooting by narrowing the scope of time/product/channel that’s maintained.
