Period to-date/to-goal aggregation

The following metrics are available in the main planning view:

  • Month TD/TG
  • Quarter TD/TG
  • Year TD/TG

Each of these metrics has two components:

  • the to-date (TD) piece is an aggregation of the actualized portion of the period such as Month To Date Sales
  • the to-go (TG) piece aggregates the remaining planned (but not actualized) portion of the period.

For example, assume a planned monthly sales goal of $100,000. If after 3 weeks there have been $65,000 in actualized sales, the Month TD value would be $65,000 and the Month TG value would be $35,000.

TD/TG metrics for non-standard periods (Half, Season, etc.) require additional configuration.
