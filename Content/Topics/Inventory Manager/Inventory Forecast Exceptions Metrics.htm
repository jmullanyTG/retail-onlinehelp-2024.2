Inventory forecast exceptions metrics

The following metrics are available in the Inventory Forecast Exceptions group. These metrics are selected or removed using the Choose Fields dialog.

Metric Definition
Inventory Forecast Current Consumption Ratio (Current Forecast Quantity - Current Rate Sales) / Current Rate Sales
Inventory Forecast Current Forecast Quantity Forecast Per Day for current week
Inventory Forecast Current Rate Sales Sales Per Day for current week
Inventory Forecast Demand History Total Sales of past [PeriodCount] periods (Excluding this week)
Inventory Forecast Forecast LY Sales Ratio (Sum of effective forecast for [PeriodCount] future periods(Excluding this period) - DemandHistory ) / DemandHistory
Inventory Forecast Forecast Sales Ratio (Sum of effective forecast for [PeriodCount] future periods(Excluding this period) - LY DemandHistory ) / LY DemandHistory
Inventory Forecast Is Current Consumption Ratio Error Current Consumption Larger than threshold
Inventory Forecast Is Forecast LY Sales Ratio Error Forecast Error LY larger than threshold
Inventory Forecast Is Forecast Sales Ratio Error Forecast Error larger than threshold
Inventory Forecast Is MAPE Error Method fit lower than the threshold
Inventory Forecast Lost Sales Detection Lost sales detected in current period
Inventory Forecast LY Demand History Sum of current and [PeriodCount-1] Last Year
Inventory Forecast MAPE Mean Absolute Percentage Error (Forecast Method Fit)
Inventory Forecast Periods Forecast Exceptions calculated by how many periods (Configuration possible)
Inventory Forecast Reviewed Reviewed Button in Forecast Manager
Inventory Forecast Reviewed Reason Autopopulated by Exception (editable column)
Inventory Forecast Sales Spike Factor Relative Deviation from the mean for the past year
Inventory Forecast Total Forecast Total of [PeriodCount] forecast excluding current
Inventory Forecast Zero Forecast Total Forecast is Zero
