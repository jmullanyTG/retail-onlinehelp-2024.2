Supply chain

The supply information for specific inventory can be viewed and managed on the Supply Chain tab. Lead time, order multiple, and both minimum and maximum order quantities can be adjusted at this level if applicable. Alternate suppliers can also be added or removed within this tab.

Allocation versus replenishment information

The Supply Chain tab displays allocation or replenishment related information depending on whether the selected item is managed by the Allocations module or the Demand Forecasting/Inventory Planning/Replenishment suite (Replenishment, for short).

If both the Allocations module and the Replenishment suite are enabled, the Allocation Strategy Selection field in the Allocation Strategy screen sets whether an item is managed by Allocations or Replenishment.

The Execution Process column can be added to the Inventory Manager view to see which process manages each item. Its read-only values are Allocations engine or Replenishment engine.
