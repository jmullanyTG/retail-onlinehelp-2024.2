Inventory status

Field Description
Availability The ability, in percentage terms, to meet current demand with currently available stock.  Calculation at an item level takes on Boolean logic i.e. either stock is available or it is not, with no in-between indications.
Days From Most Overdue Supplier Order Today’s date – open order estimated receipt date.
Days On Hand Number of days Stock on Hand quantity will last based on unconstrained demand.
Excess Stock For Redistribution Quantity SONH Qty > LT + SS + RC + Factor as a function of the forecast.
Excess Stock For Redistribution Value SONH value > LT + SS + RC + Factor as a function of the forecast.
Excess Stock Quantity Stock deemed to be in excess.
Excess Stock Value Excess Stock Quantity at cost.
Expedite Order Quantity Stock quantity contained in expedited orders.
Expedite Order Value Value of stock quantity contained in expedited order at cost.
First Expedite Order Date Suggested Date Expedite order needs to be expedited.
First Stock Out Date Estimated first date item will stock out
First Surplus Order Date Date of first surplus order
Late Orders Quantity Quantity of orders where estimated receipt date is older than today
Leftover Stock Quantity Quantity of stock left over after a store closure or end of season
Model Stock Quantity Quantity of model stock.
Model Stock Value Value of model stock at cost.
Most Overdue Supplier Order Date Today’s date – the estimated receipt date of late orders
Number Of Days To First Expedite Order Days to First Expedite Order.
Number Of Days To First Surplus Order Days to first Surplus Order
Number Of Days To Stock Out Days to first stock out date
Potential Stock Out Quantity Quantity of stock at risk of stocking out.
Potential Stock Out Value Value of potential stock out quantity at cost.
Projected Leftover Stock Quantity Quantity of projected leftover stock (such as at end of season or after a store closure)
Shortfall Stock Quantity Quantity of stock which is at risk of falling below safety stock levels.
Shortfall Stock Value Value of shortfall stock out quantity at cost.
Surplus Order Quantity Quantity of stock contained in surplus order.
Surplus Order Value Value of surplus order stock quantity at cost.
