Aggregate Forecast

The Aggregate Forecast view docked within the Inventory Manager blades displays the aggregated item master (item at all sites) values for both sales history and rolled-up effective inventory forecast throughout the whole company.

How far back historical periods are retrieved is based on system policy Maximum Number of History Days To Use (Engine: Forecasts).

The item shown is parameter driven and is based on the highlighted inventory in the inventory manager grid. The following can be viewed:

  • aggregated sales history at all sites for a particular item to understand how the item is performing throughout the whole company.
  • forecasted demand for a particular item at all sites to understand how the individual inventory or configured level forecasts are contributing to the projected sales throughout the whole company.

Supersession sales types are excluded.
