Viewing included and potential parameters

Most of the pre-loaded survey rules have a few included parameters. To view the included and potential parameters:

  1. Select a row in the Definitions tab.

  2. Click the Edit button on the toolbar.

  3. Choose the Method Surveys tab.

  4. Click the Manage Surveys button.

  5. Double click on a survey name.

    The Survey Definition window displays with both included and potential parameters.

Included parameters display in a pane on the right side above a list of potential parameters that can be included to customize a survey or create a unique survey.

A description of included and potential parameters can be viewed by hovering over the parameter name.

Users can write custom expressions make surveys more restrictive or more lenient. Double-click a potential parameter to add it to the Expression section. After parameters are selected, the actual mathematical expression that the engine uses displays in the Expression section.

Click the Test Expression button to find out if an error was made when writing the expression.
