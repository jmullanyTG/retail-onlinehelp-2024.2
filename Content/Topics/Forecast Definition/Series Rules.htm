Method series rules

A series rule is used to modify the entire series of data points (i.e. the whole data set). This constitutes a permanent change to the series. This can be used to account for scenarios such as:

  • the addition of safety stock (raising the minimum level of the series), or

  • predicting a drop of 10% for all future points.

There are several method series rules within the forecast order of operations in which the series rule may be executed. Default parameters are presented when a series rule is added and their values can be entered.

There are several series rules which can be added to a forecasting method. If selected, these rules are placed at an appropriate stage within the order of operations.

Series rule stages

There are four stages within the order of operations in which a series rule may be applied. This varies depending on the rule and the intention. It is important to understand the steps preceding and following the series rules to properly locate a series rule within the order of operations.
