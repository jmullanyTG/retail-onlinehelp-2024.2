Setting up supply chains

Use the following procedure to set up supply chains.

Creating a site or supplier supply chain

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Supply Chain.
  2. If the Open Supply Chain window displays, click Cancel to close it.

  3. From the toolbar, click the New button.

    The Create Supply Chain window displays.

  4. Enter the name and description for the supply chain.

  5. From the Supply Chain Type dropdown menu, select Site/Supplier, and click Ok.

    The supply chain document displays the imported sites as a flat supply chain.

  6. To review the override view, click the Overrides button on the toolbar.
  7. Click Switch View in the Hierarchy pane to toggle between the tree and form views.

  8. In form view, right click a columns header and select Column Chooser.

    The Customization window displays.

  9. Select all of the site groups and attributes that apply to the supply chain.

  10. In the Is A DC? column, click the filter icon to identify the sites that have been imported as designated distribution centers.

    The Hierarchy pane updates and displays sites with their respective designated distribution centers.

Cloning and editing supply chains

  • From the Maintenance tab, click Supply Chain. The Open Supply Chain window displays.

If the Open Supply Chain window does not automatically appear, click Open Supply Chain on the toolbar.

Filtering for unconfigured supply chains

Use the Is Supply Chain Configured? field in a site filter to determine if a store is the top or root node of the default supply chain. The condition must be set to False.
