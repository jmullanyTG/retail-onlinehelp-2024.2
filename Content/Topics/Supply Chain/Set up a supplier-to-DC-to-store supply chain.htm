Setting supplier-to-DC-to-store supply chains

A supplier-to-DC-to-store supply chain involves external suppliers delivering to distribution centers (DCs) which then distribute stock to stores.

All sites (stores and DCs) are on the same level in the supply chain hierarchy, and the parent source for each site is the external supplier.

To create a supplier to DC to store supply chain, it is necessary to organize the stores under their regional DCs in the hierarchy, with each store having a DC as its parent source.

After that, it is necessary to choose a second DC to be an alternate source for a store.

With a supplier to DC to store supply chain, the system:

  1. calculates projected demand versus projected stock on hand for each item at each store.
  2. calculates stock needed in each DC to meet the demand of stores stocked from that DC.
  3. generates orders from external suppliers as needed to stock the DCs with enough inventory to meet the collective store demand.
  4. generates distribution orders to deliver the right amount of inventory from DCs to stores to meet each store's projected demand.

Creating the initial supply chain

  1. Click New on the toolbar.

    The Create Supply Chain window displays.

  2. Enter a name for the supply chain and select Site/Supplier as the supply chain type.

  3. Click Ok.

    The supply chain appears on the Supply Chain screen.

    In the Hierarchy pane, the organization is flat (stores and DCs are on the same level).

  4. Click the Switch View button to change the hierarchy view to a grid.

  5. Right-click on a column header and select Column Chooser to add the Channel Description, Country Description, and Site Region Description columns to the grid.

  6. Filter the Channel Description column for the desired channel.

  7. Filter the Country Description column as required.

  8. Right-click in the grid and select Select All to select all stores.

  9. Right-click in the Parent Site column and select Multiple Changes.

    The Multi Data Change Form For Column Parent Site window displays.

  10. Select a DC in the New Value field and click Save.

  11. Click SaveIn the Supply Chain toolbar.

    The Hierarchy grid now shows the DC as the new parent site for stores.

  12. Continue changing inventory sources for other regions until all stores have their primary DC as their inventory source.

Defining alternate sources for stores

  1. In the Hierarchy panel, select a store.

    The details of the store display in the right pane.

  2. In the Alternate Source section, find the DC and enter 2 in the Priority column.

  3. Click Save.

    The DC is now set as the second source of inventory for the store.

  4. Continue assigning alternate sources for other stores.
  5. Click Reprocess to process the saved changes and rebuild the supply chain.
