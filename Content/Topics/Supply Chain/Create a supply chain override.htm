Creating supply chain overrides

An override can be created that ignores the designed supply chain for a specific set of items at specific sites and uses another source for those items.

Accessing the Open Supply Chain screen

  • Navigate to Maintenance > Supply Chain.

To create supply chain overrides

  1. In the Open Supply Chain window, select the supply chain to be overridden.

  2. In the Overrides pane at the bottom of the screen, click New.

    A new override row appears in the pane.

  3. In the Item Filter column and the Site Filter column, click the ellipsis ('...') to select the items and sites to be included in the override.

  4. Select filters and click Ok.

    The filters display in the Overrides section.

  5. Enter the start and end dates for the override.

  6. In the Override Sources pane, click New.

    A new override source row appears in the pane.

  7. In the Source Site column, click the dropdown arrow to select the sites to be included in the override.

  8. In the Source Supplier column, select the override supplier.

  9. Enter a lead time in the Lead Time Days column.
  10. Click Save in the toolbar to save the new override to the supply chain.
  11. Click Reprocess in the toolbar to process the saved changes and rebuild the supply chain.
