Reviewing direct-to-store supply chains

A direct-to-store supply chain is one in which the inventory source for a store is an external supplier.

With this supply chain, TG JE:

  1. calculates projected demand versus projected stock on hand for each item at each site.
  2. generates orders from the external supplier for each item at each location.
  3. has those orders delivered directly from the supplier to each site.

To view a supply chain supplier

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Supply Chain.

  2. Select the desired supplier using the Open Supply Chain filter and click Ok.

    The supply chain document displays.

  3. Select a store site.

    In the following example, the supply chain's source displays in the Alternate Source Name column.
