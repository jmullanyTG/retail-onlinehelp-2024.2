Modifying a specific variant's site pricing

A specific variant's site pricing can be modified to be different from the other sites in a price action. This creates a pricing exception within a product family.

To view an individual site's variant pricing within a site cluster:

  • Click Open on the Actions tab.

  • In the Details grid, select a variant and click the Variant Details button to display the Variant Details view.

    The Variant Details screen displays variant-site details.

All fields are read-only with the exception of Markup Amount. If a value is entered that is different from the price action cluster level, then the background color of the Markup Amount cell changes to denote the pricing exception at the variant level.

When a plan’s Product Pricing Level is set to Variant Site Plan, then the system policy Advanced Validation log is ignored and the Advanced Validation log displays.
