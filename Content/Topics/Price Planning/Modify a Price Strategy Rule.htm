Modify a price strategy rule

Modifications can be made directly to the Price Strategy Rule grid. Modifications cannot be made to the Rule Type and Rule Calculation fields. Modifying Price Strategy Rules causes associated Price Actions to be marked for recalculation and evaluation, unless already exported.

Click Save to save any modifications. Click Cancel to discard any modifications.

Modifying the Priority Order

Enter the new priority order value for the Price Strategy Rule to be modified and click Save. The existing priorities are re-ordered accordingly.

Priority Order 1 – 7 and #5 is modified to #2.

  • #1 will remain #1
  • #2 becomes #3
  • #3 becomes #4
  • #4 becomes #5
  • #6 stays #6
  • #7 stays #7

Modifying the Validation Severity

Select a new Severity Type from the dropdown. Selecting Error gives the ability to mark the field to allow error overrides.
