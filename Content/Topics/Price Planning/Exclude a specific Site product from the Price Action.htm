Item Site plans: Excluding or re-including an item or item location

To exclude or re-include an item or item location, select a price action in the Price Actions grid and click Open to open the price action in a separate tab. In the Details grid, select one or more items or item locations and click the Variant Details button on the Details grid toolbar.

The Variant Details view displays values at the item and item location levels.

The Items grid (top) displays the individual products across all valid sites. Modifying a value causes the system to shade the cell and to apply the modification to all sites. Site modifications can be viewed in the Item Locations grid (bottom).

Modifications may affect calculated results which will be aggregated in the Details grid as well as marking the Exception checkbox for ease of filtering.

The Item Locations grid (bottom) displays the individual products by each Site. Modifications may affect calculated results which will be aggregated in the Variants and Sites tabs of the Details grid as well as marking the Exception checkboxes for ease of filtering.

To exclude items and item locations:

  1. Select an item from the Items grid and/or the Item Locations grid and click Exclude.

    The screen refreshes and the item and associated locations are denoted by a strikethrough.

  2. Click Ok.

The item will not be included during calculation, validation evaluation nor roll ups as well as not included in the export.

To re-include items and item locations:

  1. Select an excluded item (denoted by a strikethrough) from the Items grid and/or the Item Locations grid and click Include.

    The screen refreshes and the item and associated locations are re-included.

  2. Click Ok.
