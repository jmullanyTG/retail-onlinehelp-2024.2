Variant Site plans: Excluding or re-including a variant or site

In the Pricing Plan’s Actions tab, select a price action and click Open to display the price action in a separate tab. The price action’s particulars display in the Details section.

Excluding a variant from all configured sites

To exclude a variant from all configured sites:

  1. In the Details grid, select a variant and click the Variant Details button on the toolbar to display the Variant Details view.

  2. Select the check box in the first column and click Exclude on the toolbar.

    The screen automatically refreshes and the names of excluded variants are denoted by a strikethrough.

The system will not implement any Price Strategy calculations nor validations for any products within the variant.

Re-including a variant in all configured sites

To re-include a variant to all configured sites:

  1. In the Details grid, select the variant and click the Variant Details button on the toolbar to display the Variant Details view
  2. Select the excluded variant (denoted by a strikethrough) and click Include.

    The screen automatically refreshes and the strikethrough is removed.

Excluding a site from a price action

To exclude a site:

  1. Navigate to the Sites tab in the Details section.
  2. Mark the checkbox adjacent to the site.
  3. Click Exclude.

Once the exclusion is confirmed, excluded sites are marked with a strikethrough. Excluding sites causes removal of pricing for all variants meaning that Price Strategy calculations are not implemented nor validations against products for the excluded sites.

Re-including a site in a price action

Excluded sites are denoted by a strikethrough.

  • To a re-include an excluded site, mark the checkbox adjacent to the site and click Include.
