Scenario Detail section

The actual items that were used in the calculation of each performance band in the Scenario Results section can be reviewed. If needed, selectively exclude items from the calculation.

Displaying the Scenario Detail section

Click the Scenario Detail button located on the right hand side of the Rate of Sale Calculator section.

Undocking the Scenario Detail section

Click on the header in the Scenario Detail section and select Float.

Including or excluding items from a scenario

  1. Check or uncheck the item’s checkbox in the Included field.

  2. Click Save.

    The results of the scenario with or without the selected items display.

  3. Recalculate the scenario by clicking on the Calculate button on the Rate of Sale Calculator section toolbar.
