Promotions planning

Promotions planning is provided as supplemental functionality to the main and supporting views in Assortment Planning. This functionality can:

  • estimate promotional effects on sales plans so ensuring sufficient stock to support those sales.

  • plan differences in promotional effects and time frames all the way down to the placeholder/cluster level and easily create product and site level exclusions.

  • plan multiple overlapping promotions with various interaction behaviors and best-deal logic built in for advanced planning flexibility.
