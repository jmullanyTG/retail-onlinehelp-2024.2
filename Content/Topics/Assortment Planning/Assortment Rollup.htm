Assortment rollup

The Assortment Rollup screen within Assortment Planning lets organizations aggregate their assortment plan data to the established hierarchical levels regarding products and locations for review and analysis. One of the key aspects of the Assortment Rollup is the ability to review plan versus actualized data for periods within the active assortment plan by utilizing the comparison plan functionality.

The Assortment Rollup contains all the foundational metrics related to the aggregation of sales, margin, receipt and inventory data up the product and location hierarchies defined in the active assortment plan. Future period plans and actualized historical data can be reviewed.  The aggregation of assortment plan data at established levels of the product and location hierarchies over time provides insight to an organization and helps to guide the organization in decisions around merchandising, pricing and promotions, open to buy, profitability and inventory management. 

Assortment Rollup formatting

To distinguish different metrics, the following formatting is applied:

  • Blue font, bold and italicized for determining partial aggregate period of BOP and EOP values

  • Light green (actualized) and dark green (planned) for LY fields

  • Default colors (actualized) and light purple (planned) for Comparison fields
