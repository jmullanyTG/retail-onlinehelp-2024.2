ROS scenario calculation and results

After the inputs for a scenario are set, calculation gathers the data returned by those inputs and then applies a data cleansing process to remove data points that would skew the scenario results. The cleansing process used is identical to that employed during Rationalization.

Raw ROS which has not undergone data cleansing also displays for comparison purposes. The raw ROS can also be applied if desired.

Reviewing Raw ROS and Adjusted ROS sales distributions

Once scenarios have been calculated, users can review the resulting ROS by defined performance bands (set during configuration) as both raw and adjusted (cleansed) values.  The results are grouped into the following performance percentiles (configurable during implementation):

  • Minimum
  • 25th percentile
  • Median (50th percentile)
  • 75th percentile
  • Maximum

  • Average (default selection)

    Average shows the true average ROS per variant/period for the selected data and not the average from the configured percentile bands.

Performance percentiles grid layout

The sales history for the selected items is distributed across a performance percentile (Worst, 30%, 50%, 70%, and Best). (Range is configurable during design).

Scenario results field definitions

Field Description
Raw ROS No data cleansing or filtering
Adjusted ROS Sales history that has been cleansed
Record Count The number of data points (style/color sales periods) used in the calculation of the displayed rates of sale. This serves as an indication of relative data strength.
Effective ROS The ROS to be applied to the list of placeholders/variants in the third and bottom view of the ROS calculator screen.  The Effective ROS is adjusted by checking the ROS from the Scenario results or by manually entering a value.

ROS selection is cluster specific and the distribution selection can vary by cluster.
