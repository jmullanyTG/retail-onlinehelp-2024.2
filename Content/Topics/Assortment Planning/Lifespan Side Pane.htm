Lifespan side pane

While in Placeholder or Collection view, open the Lifespan side pane by selecting it from the View Options toolbar menu or by right-clicking on a column header and choosing Lifespan.

In the pane, perform the following actions:

  • lengthen or shorten an item lifespan by clicking on its phase-in or phase-out period and dragging it to the desired period.

  • move an entire lifespan earlier or later by right-clicking it and dragging it to the desired period.

To adjust or move multiple lifespans bars, press CTRL and then drag the selected lifespans.

The Lifespan pane shows the entire life cycle of each item over time including: phase-in, phase-out, markdown periods, and any non-selling periods.

Color Description
Blue The planned selling period for an item.
Purple The markdown period for an item.
Light gray A partial non-selling period.
Dark gray A non-selling period.
