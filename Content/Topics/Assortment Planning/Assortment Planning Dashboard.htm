Dashboard

The Assortment Planning dashboard helps organizations view and work with aggregated data to manage the business and drive sales and profits.

Assortment plans contain granular data related to the item plans of an organization, as it represents the detail behind what items are being bought, when items deliver, and the life cycle information for items related to Sales, Cost of Sales, Margin, Receipts, and Inventory ownership. This information can be leveraged to view plans at various aggregated levels of the product, location and time hierarchies.

The aggregation of assortment plan data at established levels of the product and location hierarchies over time provides insight to an organization and helps to guide the organization in decisions around merchandising, pricing and promotions, open to buy, profitability and inventory management. Additionally the utilization of attributes can also provide various insights into an assortment to understand the trends and selling patterns.

The dashboard is accessed by navigating to Plan > Assortment Planning.

Report dashboards

There are six report dashboards each containing information specific to Assortment Planning.

The report dashboards are: Assortment Buy Sheet Report, Assortment Choice Count By Period Report, Assortment Choice Count Report, Assortment Upcoming Commitments Dashboard Report, Assortment By Site Report, and Receipt Constraint Comparison Report.

The first three tabs are utilized to aggregate the item level plans to provide more holistic views as it relates to key measures contained within the Assortment Planning module. The fourth is used to track upcoming due dates for key milestones in the commitment process for orders generated from assortment plans.
