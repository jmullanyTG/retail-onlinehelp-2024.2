Apply Rate of Sale section

In the Apply Rate of Sale section, the results of a Rate of Sale scenario are applied to the placeholders and collections selected in the main planning view. After applying the results, the sales plans for the selected placeholders and collections are adjusted to reflect the ROS values that have been applied.

This section should already be populated based on the selected lines in the main planning view. If not, click Execute to update the view.

Applying rates of sale

  1. In the Scenario Results section, check the boxes corresponding to the rates of sale to be applied to the selected placeholder/variant/cluster combinations.

  2. In the Apply Rate of Sale section, check the Grand Total checkbox to make the selection eligible for the ROS values selected in the Scenario Results section.
  3. Click Copy Rate of Sale at the top of the Apply Rate of Sale section to populate the selected ROS values from the Scenario Results section.

  4. Click Save.

    Before clicking Save, clear any copied ROS values by clicking Undo on the Apply Rate of Sale toolbar.
