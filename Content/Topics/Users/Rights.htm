Rights

Within the Users module, Rights can be either configured or associated or disassociated with users.

Adding or removing rights

  1. Click Users in the Navigation Menu.

  2. Select Assign Rights.

    The User Information window displays.

  3. In the Rights tab, click the > and < buttons to add or remove rights for users.

  4. Click Ok.

    The rights are updated.

Configuring rights

  1. Click Rights in the Navigation Menu.
  2. Select the right to be configured.

  3. Click Configure Rights in the Navigation Menu

    The Right Information window displays. A description of the right is available for viewing in the Right Detail tab.

  4. Include or exclude Roles and Memberships in the appropriate tabs.
  5. Click Ok.

Including or excluding Rights using the Rights tab

Rights can be included or excluded within the Users and Roles section of the Users module using the Rights tab. The Rights tab displays the currently included and excluded rights associated with the user. Only those Rights available for selection display in the Rights tab.
