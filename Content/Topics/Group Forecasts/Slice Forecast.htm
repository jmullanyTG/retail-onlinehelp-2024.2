Forecasting selected slices of models

In the Group Forecasting solution, a selected slice of the model can be forecast. Slices are a vertical hierarchy built as a part of the model and are anchored around each item at the top of a static hierarchy. Slices can be forecasted individually to improve the overall performance of the Group Forecasting solution.

The Forecast button, under Hierarchy Navigation on the Group Forecast screen, forecasts the entire slice associated with the highlighted node.

The Lock button locks only the highlighted node.

When defining a node, that node must begin at the highest level in the model. A node is the combination of two identification values: the SliceID and SliceLevelID (both based on quint keys).
