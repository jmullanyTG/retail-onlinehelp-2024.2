Hierarchy navigation

The Hierarchy Navigation section describes the included data and levels for the model. This section is the main navigation pane for the other group forecasting data screens. Highlighted levels display their data in the Current Hierarchy Level – Data and Current Hierarchy Level – Details grids.

The Hierarchy Navigation pane can be toggled on and off using the Hierarchy button in the ribbon.

The included levels and their respective children display in the navigation pane.

Locking and unlocking hierarchy levels

Forecasts can be locked and unlocked at certain levels.

  1. In the Hierarchy navigation pane, click the Lock icon.

    The Forecast Task dialog displays.

  2. Select one or more method sets.

  3. Click Ok.

    The locked level displays a lock icon.

To unlock a level, click the Unlock icon.

The Unlock icon only displays when a locked level is selected.

Pushing forecasts to related inventories

Click the Push icon to split forecasts to related inventories.

Removing forecasts from related inventories

Click the Revoke icon to remove forecasts from related inventories
