Group Forecasts Overview

The Group Forecasts module is used to build forecast models at hierarchical levels that can be drilled down to lower levels for use as projected demand.

Forecast group model management

Group forecasts are generated by building a model, which is then processed by the forecasting engine to provide a statistical forecast or projected demand at a hierarchical level.

Group forecast models must be carefully built because incorrect choices can inappropriately skew the results and affect many interdependent items and locations.

The available sales types that can be selected when building models are the listed types in tbl_SalesType.
